Our values

Accessibility first
Academic help should never depend on what your family can afford. Every session we offer is completely free, no strings attached.
Peer understanding
Students who recently learned something often explain it better than anyone. Our student tutors get it because they have been there.
No judgment

Expert guidance
Alongside our student tutors, our teacher tutors bring years of classroom experience to give students the depth they need for exams.

Advice from tutors

Study smarter, not harder.

🧠
Spaced repetition
Review material at increasing intervals rather than cramming the night before. Anki makes this effortless.
✏️
Teach it back
Explain a concept out loud as if teaching someone else. If you cannot, you have not learned it yet.
📵
Single-tasking
One subject per session. Phone in another room. 45 focused minutes beats three distracted hours.
❓
Ask early
Do not wait until the night before your mock. Confusion compounds, address it in the first week.
